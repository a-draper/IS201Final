6 Ways to Improve Communication with Your Child

  1. Create a Climate for Open Communication
    • Ask open-ended questions
    • Don't force communication
    • Make sure environment is loving, understanding & accepting
  2. Active Listening
    • Give full attention
    • Don't cut off or interrupt
    • Help them work through their thoughts & feelings
  3. Validation
    • Tell them you understand how they feel
    • Convey that it makes sense why they feel this way
    • This helps kids feel seen & heard
  4. Avoid speech-making, problem solving & overreating
    • Speeches don't motivate change and aren't effective
    • Kids don't want you to solve their problems
    • Overreacting discourages communication
  5. Model Positive Communication
    • Practice these principles of positive communication
    • Avoid being judgmental, overly sarcastic, manipulative or playing the victim in your communication
    • What you show, is what they know!
  6. Repair
    • Quick apology letting them know you are sorry without making excuses for your actions
    • The goal of this conversation is reconnection
    • This replaces the story of self-blame with one of love an acceptance
